How to play

Land on a white landing pad. Go slow and straight. If you touch down too fast or angled, you crash. Try flipping for bonus points.

Try the controls

  • Up arrow
  • Left arrow
  • Right arrow
  • Hold the up arrow while pressing the left or right arrows
  • Tap the center of the screen
  • Tap the left side
  • Tap the right side
  • Hold down on the center while you tap the sides
